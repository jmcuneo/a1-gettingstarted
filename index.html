These are the steps to salvation through the gospel:

  1. Acknowledge Sin: Recognize falling short of God's standards (Romans 3:23).
  2. Believe in Jesus: Trust in Jesus as the Son of God who died and rose for your sins (John 3:16; Romans 5:8).
  3. Repent and Confess: Turn from sin, confess faith in Jesus as Lord (Acts 3:19; Romans 10:9-10).
  4. Invite Jesus In: Welcome Jesus into your life as Lord (Revelation 3:20).
  5. Receive God's Gift: Accept salvation as a gift through faith (Ephesians 2:8-9).
  6. Start a Relationship: Begin a personal relationship with God through prayer and reading the Bible.
  7. Get Baptized: Make an outward confession of faith.
  8. Grow in Faith: Continuously grow in faith, obedience, and love.
